Our Story

College Sahayak was born from a simple yet powerful observation: polytechnic students across Maharashtra were struggling to access quality study materials, often relying on outdated resources or incomplete notes shared through informal networks.

Our founder, Arjun, experienced this challenge firsthand during his diploma studies. After successfully completing his engineering degree and gaining experience in the tech industry, he decided to solve this problem for future generations of students.

What started as a small project to digitize and organize study materials quickly grew into a comprehensive platform. Today, College Sahayak serves thousands of students across multiple engineering branches, providing them with verified, up-to-date resources that align with the latest MSBTE curriculum.

Our team combines deep understanding of the polytechnic education system with cutting-edge technology to create solutions that truly make a difference in students' academic journeys.
